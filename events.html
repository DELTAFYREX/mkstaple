Facebook for Business Seminar - Aug 15

Facebook's Boost Your Business event took place in St. John's, Newfoundland, and was a session dedicated to teaching local small business owners how to optimize their presence on the world's largest social network. The event was co-hosted by Startup Newfoundland & Labrador and the St. John's Board of Trade, with special guests Seamus O'Regan, MP for St. John's South-Mount Pearl, Brent Homan, Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada, and Mark Matz, Public Safety Canada.
